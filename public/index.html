About the beta programme

Ancient and modern.
Traditional teachings and scientific research.
The same coordinates.

You do not have to choose between these traditions. A person can hold a scientific worldview and still find meaning in ancient calendrics. A practising astrologer can be rigorous about evidence. What CDP offers is not a compromise between these positions but a genuine synthesis of them: every insight available in both the language of tradition and the language of research, your choice.

Every planetary position in CDP is calculated from Swiss Ephemeris data derived from NASA's JPL DE431 ephemeris. Every moon phase is verified against USNO tables. The 260-day Mayan count is grounded in peer-reviewed archaeoastronomy (Šprajc, Inomata and Aveni 2023, Science Advances). The Dreamspell layer is clearly labelled as a modern 20th-century system, distinct from the ancient K'iche' tradition.

The science layer rests on a mechanism that research across many fields has confirmed: what we hold in mind, we attend to, and therefore notice and experience more fully. Where a spiritual teaching says we draw toward us what we hold, cognitive science says we selectively attend to it. The outcome is the same. This is not a single researcher's insight. It runs through decades of work in selective attention, predictive processing, circadian cognition, the neuroscience of emotion, attachment theory, depth psychology and more. The reading does not tell you what will happen. It gives you a frame that shapes what you are able to perceive.
